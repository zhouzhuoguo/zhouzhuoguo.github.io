I received my B.S. in Communication Engineering from the School of Electronic and Information Engineering, Beijing Jiaotong University (Haidian, Beijing) in June 2025. My research interest is energy-efficient AI chips for edge computing via cross-layer innovations, spanning from algorithms to architecture and circuit design. Beginning in January 2025, I worked remotely with Dr. Changchun Zhou (Postdoctoral researcher) on AI chip projects. In summer 2025, I worked on-site at Duke University with Dr. Zhou, leading hardware modeling of Diffusion and PTV2 (PointTransformerV2) networks under different hardware configurations.
