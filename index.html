I received my B.S. in Communication Engineering from the School of Electronic and Information Engineering, Beijing Jiaotong University (Haidian, Beijing) in June 2025. In summer 2025, I studied AI-chip–related topics at Duke University with Dr. Changchun Zhou (Postdoctoral Researcher), leading simulation modeling of Diffusion and PTV2 networks under specific hardware configurations.

Research

🚀 Chip Design for AI Acceleration

面向主流 AI 模型（Diffusion、Transformers、CNN/Point 等）的定制芯片与加速器设计。

  • 跨层优化：从算法 → 架构 → 电路
  • 能耗/带宽/存储建模与权衡
  • 例：本项目在相同精度下实现 ×N 的吞吐提升与 ×M 的能效提升

🌐 Online AI Deployment Systems

软硬件协同的在线推理系统：FPGA/自研集群/云端平台。

  • 硬件友好型算法与编译映射（如 MLIR → 指令）
  • 调度与多任务分配

⚡ Impact

目标是在主流 AI 任务上实现 10×–1000× 的成本/功耗优势，同时保持同等精度与速度。
